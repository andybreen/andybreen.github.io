Frequently Asked Questions

What types of plans do you offer?
We offer a variety of mobile and broadband plans to suit your needs. Our mobile plans range from basic talk and text packages to high-data plans with unlimited calls. Our broadband plans include fiber optic and wireless options with various speeds and data allowances. Visit our Plans page for details.
How do I check my mobile data usage?
You can easily check your data usage through our ACME-Tel app or by logging into your account on our website. You can also dial *123# (example short code) from your mobile phone.
What should I do if my broadband connection is down?
First, try restarting your modem and router. If the issue persists, please contact our customer support hotline at 1800-ACME-TEL (example number) or use our live chat support on our website.
How can I pay my bill?
We offer several convenient payment options, including online banking, credit card payments, GIRO, and AXS stations. You can find more information about payment methods on our Billing page.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team by phone at 1800-ACME-TEL, via email at support@acmetel.com (example email), or through our live chat on our website.
